title: "My training plans"
description: "Access the workout routines your coach has designed for you."
---

The **Mis Rutinas de Entrenamiento** page shows all training plans available to you. These include plans shared with all students as well as plans your coach has assigned specifically to you.

## Plan types

When your plans load, each one is labeled with a badge indicating its scope:

| Type | Badge | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Plan Global** | Blue badge | Designed for all students in the gym. Every student sees these plans. |
| **Plan Personal** | Green badge | Assigned to you individually by your coach. Only you can see it. |

The backend filters plans automatically using your authentication token — you only receive the global plans and the personal plans assigned to your account.

## Plan header information

Each plan card shows:

- **Title** — the name of the training plan
- **Designer** — the coach who created it, shown as `Diseñado por: [name]`. Global plans default to `USM Fitness` if no specific coach name is set.
- **Expiry date** — the date until which the plan is valid (`Válido hasta: DD/MM/YYYY`)

## Exercise cards

Within each plan, exercises are displayed in a grid. Each exercise card shows:

- **Muscle group tag** — a label in the top-right corner of the card indicating the targeted muscle group (e.g., `PECHO`, `PIERNAS`, `ESPALDA`)
- **Exercise number** — a sequential index within the plan
- **Exercise name** — the full name of the exercise
- **Sets, reps, and rest** — displayed in a three-column grid:
  - **Series** — number of sets
  - **Reps** — number of repetitions per set
  - **Descanso** — rest time in seconds between sets

### Video tutorials

If an exercise has a tutorial video linked, a **Ver Video Tutorial** button appears at the bottom of the card. Clicking it opens the video in a new tab.

Not all exercises have video links — the button only appears when a `video_url` is set for that exercise.

## Empty plan

If a plan exists but the coach has not yet added exercises to it, the plan card shows:

> El entrenador aún no ha añadido ejercicios a este plan.

<Note>
  If the page shows no plans at all, your coach has not yet assigned any routines to your account. Contact your trainer directly and ask them to create a plan for you using the [routine manager](/features/routine-manager). Global plans are visible to all students, so if none appear, the gym may not have any published yet.
</Note>
