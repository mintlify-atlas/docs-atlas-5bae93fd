Gimnasio USM is the official gym management platform for Universidad Santa María. It connects students, coaches, and administrators through a unified interface designed around how the USM gym actually operates — from session booking to personalized training routines.

Whether you're a student reserving your next training block, a coach managing attendance and building custom routines, or an admin controlling the weekly schedule, this platform has a role-specific experience tailored for you.

## How roles work

Gimnasio USM uses three user roles, each with a different set of features:

| Role | Who | Access |
|------|-----|--------|
| **Student (Alumno)** | Gym members | Book sessions, view routines, manage fitness profile |
| **Coach (Entrenador)** | Gym trainers | Attendance, routine builder, student records |
| **Admin** | Gym administrators | All coach features + user management + full schedule control |

<Note>
  Your role is assigned by an administrator. Contact your gym administrator if you need a role change or if you're a new coach or admin joining the platform.
</Note>
